BTS

Bangtan Sonyeodan 방탄소년단

Bangtan Sonyeodan is a South Korean boy band.It was formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The band consists of seven members:Kim Namjoon, Kim Seokjin, Min Yoongi, Jung Hoseok, Park Jimin, Kim Taeyung and Jeon Jungkook.BTS debuted on June 13,2013 with 'No more dream'. Their first album was 2 Cool 4 School.

Kim Namjoon

RM

Kim Namjoon known as RM is the leader of BTS. He was the first member to be recruited. RM is the main rapper, a songwriter and record producer. RM was born on September 12, 1994. He is 24 years old. He was born in Goyang,South Korea.

Kim Seokjin

Jin

Kim Seokjin known as Jin is the oldest member of BTS. He is a vocalist and songwriter. Jin was born on December 4, 1994. He is currently 25 yrs. old. Jin was born in Gyeonggi-do,South Korea.

Min Yoongi

Suga

Min Yoongi is known a Suga but he is also known as August D. He is rapper, songwriter and record producer. Suga was born on March 9, 1993. He is currently 25 years old. Suga was born in Daegu, South Korea.

Jung Hoseok

J-Hope

Jung Hoseok is known as J-Hope. He is a rapper, dancer, songwriter, and record producer. J-Hope was born on February 18, 1994. He is currently 24 years old. J-Hope was born in Gwangju, South Korea.

Park Jimin

Jimin

Park Jimin known as Jimin. He is a vocalist and dancer. He was born on October 13, 1995. He is currently 23 years old. He was born in Busan, South Korea.

Kim Taehyung

V

Kim Taehyung known as V. He is a vocalist, actor, and songwriter. He was born on December 30, 1995. He is currently 22 years old. He was born in Daegu, South Korea.

Jeon Jungkook

Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook know as Jungkook. Jungkook is the Golden Maknae menaing he is the youngest of BTS. He is a vocalist, songwriter, record producer and video editor.

About their music

Their genre is K-pop, R&B, EDM and Hip Hop. BTS currently has 17 albums. The significance behind the music is unbelievable. A story was created through out the releases of the albums.
